## The Shape-Shifting Combat System

> **💡 Tip:** Experiment with "Active Swapping" during boss fights. Switching characters at the exact moment an ability lands can often bypass recovery frames, significantly increasing your DPS.

## Rogue-lite Progression and Run Mechanics

The game utilizes a dual-progression system that keeps the gameplay loop fresh. Even without **morbid metal co op** to share the burden, the permanent upgrades ensure that you are constantly getting stronger. You earn currency during your runs through various biomes, such as the "Sublime Garden," which can be spent on both temporary buffs and permanent skill tree nodes.

### Temporary vs. Permanent Upgrades

| Upgrade Type | Description | Persistence |
|---|---|---|
| **Blessings** | Random buffs found after clearing an area | Lost upon death |
| **Echoes/Flex** | Specific combat abilities or stat boosts | Lost upon death |
| **Skill Tree** | Core stat increases and new combo unlocks | **Permanent** |
| **Boss Abilities** | Unique powers harvested from defeated bosses | **Permanent** |

The "Mach 2" (or 2.0) versions of enemies represent a significant difficulty spike. These foes require players to have a deep understanding of their current build synergies. Because you cannot rely on a **morbid metal co op** partner to revive you, positioning and the use of defensive perks—like the starting barrier—are vital for survival.

## Navigating the Sublime Garden

The "Sublime Garden" serves as one of the primary biomes in the 2026 version of the game. It is a randomized environment where players must choose between the "right path" to progress and hidden "discovery places" that house rare unlocks. These secret areas often contain the most powerful rogue-lite elements, such as dramatic buffs for your support drone.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Currency earned during a run is discarded if you die before reaching a rest area. Always prioritize health upgrades if you are carrying a large amount of unspent points.

The drone acts as a pseudo-partner in the absence of **morbid metal co op**. It can be upgraded to provide:
*   Automated fire support.
*   Shield regeneration.
*   Enemy debuffs (slowing down time).
*   Resource collection.

## Combat Mastery: Boss Encounters

Bosses in this world are designed to test the limits of your reaction time. The first major boss encounter introduces unblockable laser shots and area-of-effect slams that require precise jumping and dodging. This rhythmic combat is the heart of the experience, making every run feel unique even if you are tackling the same boss for the fiftieth time.
