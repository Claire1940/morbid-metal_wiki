In the current 2026 meta, players must prioritize high-threat targets like the Hammerhead mobs and Healers. Healers can sustain an entire squad of enemies indefinitely, making them your primary target in any engagement.

## Navigating the Act 2 Branches

Once you reach the central hub of Act 2, you will find the path to the Prophet Slayer blocked. The game requires you to "Complete Two Branches" before the boss arena becomes accessible. These branches are long, linear paths filled with platforming challenges over acid and intense combat arenas.

1.  **The Left Branch:** Focuses heavily on environmental hazards. You will need to jump across moving platforms while avoiding toxic liquid that drains your health rapidly.
2.  **The Right Branch:** This path is combat-intensive, featuring multiple "Emporium" stops where you can spend mana chips on health and upgrades.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the side rooms in these branches. While many crates are empty, you can occasionally find Level 3 upgrades that significantly boost your damage or health pool.

## The Prophet Slayer: Boss Strategy Guide

The Prophet is the final hurdle of the current Early Access build. This boss is a ranged specialist who utilizes heat-seeking orbs and area-of-effect (AoE) strikes. Defeating him requires a mix of aggressive damage and defensive patience.

### Phase 1: The Ranged Duel
The Prophet will hover at the edges of the arena, firing heat-seeking orbs. You must run in circles to avoid these; dodging too early will result in the orbs tracking your new position. Wait for the Prophet to dash toward you. This is his most vulnerable moment. If you can parry or dodge this dash, he will be paralyzed on the ground for several seconds.

### Phase 2: The Pillar Mechanic
The Prophet will become immune and summon pillars. He will also summon minions that link to him, sustaining his shield.
*   **Hide behind pillars:** When the Prophet prepares a massive dash, position yourself so a pillar is between you and the boss. He will crash into the pillar, stunning himself.
*   **Clear the minions:** You cannot damage the boss while his tethers are active. Use Vector's ultimate to gather and destroy the minions quickly.

### Phase 3: Survival Mode
In the final phase, the Prophet becomes increasingly erratic, filling the screen with "Heavenly Strikes." Interestingly, the Prophet will eventually begin taking damage from his own over-exertion or environmental feedback. Your primary goal here is **survival**. Focus entirely on dodging and staying mobile. If you have any "Leak" (bleed) debuffs equipped, apply them once and then focus on evasion.

## Advanced Combat Tips for 2026

To truly master this **morbid metal ekku guide**, you need to be aware of the technical nuances of the game. As of the latest 2026 updates, the game still features a few bugs that can end a run prematurely.

*   **Combo Dodge Glitch:** Be careful when switching operators mid-combo. There is a known issue where the dodge command may not register immediately after a shape-shift. Always finish your animation before attempting a critical dodge.
*   **Ultimate Conservation:** Do not waste your ultimate abilities on standard mobs unless you are at low health. You will need a full charge for the Prophet's second phase.
*   **The Leak Strategy:** Equipping upgrades that provide "Leak" or damage-over-time is highly recommended for boss fights. This allows you to deal damage while focusing 100% of your attention on avoiding the Prophet's projectiles.
