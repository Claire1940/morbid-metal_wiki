## The Core Mechanics of Morbit Metal

At its heart, **morbit metal** is a combo-driven experience that rewards aggressive play and quick reflexes. Unlike traditional hack-and-slash games where you might stick to one character for an entire level, this title introduces a revolutionary "form-switching" mechanic. You can control up to three distinct characters simultaneously, switching between them mid-combat to maintain momentum and exploit enemy weaknesses.

The simulation is designed to be punishing but rewarding. Each time you enter the fray, the world reshapes itself. Layouts shift, enemy compositions change, and the rewards you find will vary wildly. This procedural nature ensures that no two runs feel identical, forcing you to adapt your strategy on the fly.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Think of character switching as switching weapon types in a tactical shooter. Each form has a specific "effective range" and utility that can turn the tide of a battle.

## Mastering the Character Swap System

The true depth of **morbit metal** lies in the "Chain Attack" system. By timing your character swaps perfectly, you can trigger follow-up attacks that deal significantly more damage than standard strikes. For example, you might start an encounter with Vector to dash into a group of enemies, immediately switch to Flux to lock them in a stun-loop, and then swap to Ekko to deliver a crushing blow that clears the room.

### How to Build a Successful Combo

1.  **Initiation:** Use a high-mobility character to engage.
2.  **Building:** Use fast-attacking forms to build the combo multiplier.
3.  **Execution:** Swap to your heavy hitter when the enemy's posture is broken.
4.  **Rotation:** Immediately swap back to a defensive or mobile form to avoid retaliation.

| Upgrade Type | Description | Risk Level |
