One of the most anticipated features in the 2026 DLC roadmap is the expansion of the "Shift-Combo" system. ### How to Master Shift-Combos

1.  **Initiate with a Launcher:** Use a heavy character to knock enemies into the air.
2.  **Shift to Aerial Specialist:** Swap to a high-mobility form to deal damage while the enemy is airborne.
3.  **Execute the Finisher:** Shift to your highest DPS character to slam the enemy back into the ground for AOE (Area of Effect) damage.
4.  **Manage Cooldowns:** Ensure your swap-meter is recharged by picking up energy orbs dropped by defeated foes.
