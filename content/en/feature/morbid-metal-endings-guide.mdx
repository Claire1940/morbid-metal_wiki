## Strategies for Reaching the Ending

Reaching the final boss is the most difficult part of following this **morbid metal endings guide**. To ensure you can survive the gauntlet, you must optimize your build and playstyle.

### Character Synergy
You have four characters at your disposal. Swapping between them isn't just for style; it's essential for survival. One character might excel at shield-breaking, while another provides the high-DPS needed to burn through a boss's second phase.

### Upgrade Priority
Focus your resources on the following areas to improve your chances:
*   **Health Regeneration**: Essential for long runs where healing items are scarce.
*   **Swap Speed**: Reducing the cooldown on character switching allows for more fluid combos.
*   **Damage Multipliers**: Specifically those that reward aggressive play, as the game's "struggle" theme is reflected in its mechanics.

### The "Survive" Counsel
The overseer gives one final counsel: "Survive." In gameplay terms, this means prioritizing evasion over greedy damage. Most players fail the final boss because they attempt to finish the fight too quickly, falling victim to the boss's desperation attacks.
