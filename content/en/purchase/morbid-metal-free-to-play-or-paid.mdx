## The Rogue-lite Loop and Progression

Because Morbid Metal is a paid game, the progression is tuned for player satisfaction rather than "grind-to-skip" mechanics. Every run provides "Combat Data" that you can use to upgrade your permanent stats back at the hub.

### Permanent Upgrades vs. Temporary Buffs
During a run, you will find temporary modifications that alter how your abilities work. These might add elemental damage (like fire or electricity) or change the behavior of your projectiles. However, the true power comes from the meta-progression system.

1.  **Collect Resources:** Defeat bosses and clear arenas to gather rare ores.
2.  **Upgrade the Hub:** Spend resources to unlock new starting items and better healing efficiency.
3.  **Refine Characters:** Each of the four slots in your squad can be individually leveled up to unlock new passive traits.

> **Warning:** Death in Morbid Metal is permanent for that specific run. You will lose all temporary buffs, but you always keep the currency earned for permanent upgrades.
