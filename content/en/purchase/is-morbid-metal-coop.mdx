## Roguelite Progression and Upgrades

Since the game is a roguelite, your progress is measured in "runs." You move through various procedural environments, clearing rooms of enemies to earn rewards. These rewards often come in the form of substantial upgrades that can alter your playstyle for the remainder of the run.

### Upgrade Types
Upgrades can affect your entire team or specific characters within your roster. This creates a strategic layer where you must decide whether to build one "super-character" or maintain a balanced team.

*   **Damage Leech:** A percentage of damage dealt is returned as health.
*   **Attack Power Buffs:** Flat increases to your damage output.
*   **Range Extensions:** Increases the reach of certain melee attacks.
*   **Cooldown Reduction:** Allows for more frequent use of powerful skills.

> **Warning:** Because all characters share the same health pool, a mistake made while playing a slow character can jeopardize your entire run. Be careful when switching to heavier, less mobile units in high-pressure situations.
