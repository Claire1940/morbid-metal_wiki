### Advanced Combat Tips
When using the **morbid metal discord link** to research builds, you will notice that veteran players emphasize "Leak" damage. This status effect acts as a bleed debuff, slowly draining an enemy's health while you focus on dodging. This is particularly effective against the high-mobility bosses found in the second act.

*   **Master the Deflect:** Timing your dash to coincide with the red indicator above an enemy's head allows for a powerful counter-attack.
*   **Nano-Chip Management:** Use your Nano-chips at the Vitality Fabricator to restore health before a boss fight rather than gambling on random drops.
*   **Protocol Synergy:** Use Vector's Magnetic Pull to gather enemies, then swap to Echo for a heavy area-of-effect (AoE) slam.

## Boss Strategy: Uriel and The Prophet

The bosses in Morbid Metal are designed to be "rage-inducing" but fair. Each boss typically features three distinct phases, requiring players to adapt their strategy mid-fight.

### Uriel (The First Challenge)
Uriel focuses on ground slams and shockwaves. In Phase 1, he is vulnerable after his leap attack. By Phase 3, he begins charging high-damage energy fields.
*   **Strategy:** Jump over the ground waves rather than dashing through them.
*   **Opening:** Wait for the "chest beat" animation to unleash your ultimate.

### The Prophet (The Second Challenge)
The Prophet is a ranged-heavy boss who utilizes heat-seeking orbs and pillars. This fight is a test of patience and survival.
*   **Phase 1:** Hide behind the stone pillars when he charges his dash. If he hits a pillar, he becomes stunned.
*   **Phase 3:** The Prophet becomes immune to direct damage. You must survive his "Heavenly Strike" and minions while his health slowly drains from his own internal deconstruction.

> **⚠️ Warning:** There are currently no checkpoints in the Early Access version of Morbid Metal. Dying at a boss means restarting from Iteration 1.
