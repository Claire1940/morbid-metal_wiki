## Progressing Through the Broken World

As a roguelike, Morbid Metal features a procedurally generated world. Every time you die, you return to the central hub, but you carry forward "Shattered Memories" used to purchase permanent upgrades. These upgrades can range from increased health pools to entirely new combat abilities for your metal forms.

1.  **Collect Resources**: Defeated enemies drop scrap and data cores.
2.  **Upgrade the Hub**: Use scrap to unlock new facilities that provide passive buffs.
3.  **Unlock New Forms**: Discover blueprints in hidden areas of the map to expand your roster.
4.  **Defeat the Guardians**: Each biome is guarded by a massive mechanical boss that tests your mastery of the shapeshifting system.

The **Morbid Metal's release date trailer** briefly touches upon the upgrade interface, showing a complex web of enhancements that allow players to tailor their playstyle. Whether you prefer a "glass cannon" build or a tanky bruiser, the 2026 Early Access version provides ample depth to support various strategies.
