## Roguelite Elements: Upgrades, Buffs, and Curses

Every run in this simulation is unique. The world reshapes itself, altering the layout of the biomes and the types of enemies you will encounter. This procedural generation ensures that players cannot simply memorize a path to the end. Instead, you must adapt your strategy based on the upgrades and modifiers presented to you.

The **morbid metal gameplay trailer** highlights a risk-reward system involving buffs and curses. While some choices offer straightforward power increases, others might grant immense strength at a significant cost, such as reduced health or increased enemy aggression.

### Strategic Choice Comparison

| Choice Type | Potential Benefit | Potential Risk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character Upgrades** | Enhanced damage or ability cooldowns. | Usually requires specific resources. |
| **Buffs** | Passive bonuses to speed or critical hit chance. | Often temporary or biome-specific. |
| **Curses** | Massive power spikes or unique combat modifiers. | Can make survival significantly more difficult. |
| **Rewards** | New character unlocks or permanent AI growth. | Often locked behind difficult elite encounters. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be cautious when stacking multiple curses. While the power boost is tempting, the cumulative negative effects can end a promising run during a boss fight.

The simulation is divided into distinct biomes, each described as both "beautiful and brutal." These environments serve as more than just backdrops; they influence how you fight. At the end of every biome sits a boss. These are dramatic, high-stakes encounters that serve as the ultimate test of your mastery over the character-swapping mechanic. Since the **morbid metal gameplay trailer** dropped, fans have noted the scale of these bosses, which require players to identify patterns and switch characters strategically to avoid lethal damage.

### Boss Fight Checklist
*   **Identify Patterns**: Observe the boss's telegraphs before committing to a long combo.
